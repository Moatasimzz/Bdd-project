Designed for a community of Businesses and Innovators

BDD is built on five solid foundations

Community

An environment catering to creative minds, providing the framework for ambitious and sustainable communities. BDD creates a community for collaboration, facilitates the sharing of ideas and connects decision makers with entrepreneurs.

Sharing Economy

A business district providing value added services and cutting edge facilities for developing, producing and exchanging innovative tools and applications.

Smart Design

A futuristic urban planning concept, incorporating green spaces, pedestrian walkways, and modern infrastructure, ensuring a healthy and sustainable environment.

Innovation

A hub for innovation, providing access to cutting edge technologies, research and development facilities, and a platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas.

Connectivity

A state-of-the-art infrastructure, providing high speed internet connectivity, and a seamless communication network, ensuring efficient collaboration and exchange of ideas.
